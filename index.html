WHAT IS IT?

>

NAD stands for Nicotinamide Adenine Dinucleotide. It's a natural molecule found within every living cell of your body. We depend upon NAD to convert the nutrients in our food into cellular capacity and to activate natural self-repair pathways that keep our cells in good working order.

Scientific studies have demonstrated that boosting NAD results in reversal of age-related damage back to a more youthful state. A growing body of scientific research now suggests one of the best ways to combat aging is to maintain high levels of NAD as you age.

HOW DOES IT WORK?

>

Grey Matter in the brain, responsible for learning and memory, can decline up to 20% in your adult years, even if you maintain a healthy lifestyle. As we age, our brains shrink in volume, memory fades, and lesions develop. It is not all negative nor passive, as biological aging is not tied absolutely to chronological ageing. It is possible to slow biological aging and even reduce risk of age-related decline.

In order to allow our natural self-repairs systems to restore the efficiency of our cells, we must maintain and raise our NAD levels within the cells. This can be done by the foundational elements of sleep, diet, and exercise. Clockwise is just an addition to that to naturally nudging your system to produce more NAD.
